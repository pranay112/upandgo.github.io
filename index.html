About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver low cost, quality food to urban poor community with limited food access.
By purchasing staple goods at wholesale prices and delivering daily to conveniently located drop locations.
A more affordable alternative to shopping at local grocers.

Where are we ?

Initially we will serve neighborhoods in the Chicago south side:
Auburn Gresham, Beverly, Douglas, Englewood, Fuller Park, Grand Boulevard, Kenwood, Oakland, Pullman, Roseland, South Shore, Washington Park, West Englewood, and Woodlawn.
Our deliveries will be made to churches and community centers, as well as directly to a number of affordable housing properties in the target neighborhoods.

Why Us?

To eliminate food deserts in urban areas.
Individuals with fixed and low incomes will benefit from our below retail food prices as well as our selection of real, whole foods that will help promote health and wellness.
Individuals with limited access to transportation will benefit by having our products delivered to convenient locations near their home.
