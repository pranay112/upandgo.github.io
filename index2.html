About Us

Up and Go is a IIT student venture to encourage and help student entreprenuers. There are several incubators in Chicago. Up and Go is a platform that unites all the incubators to encourage collabration amongst start-ups. Up and go also aims to serve as forum for open dialouge between student entreprenuers.
